This app deploys a Logistic Regression model to predict drug use. It was created with the Monitoring the Future dataset, a study funded by the Substances Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration to study trends in teenage use and attitudes towards drugs.

Every year, approximately 11,000-16,000 students across 133 schools in the United States are given a survey that asks questions about their past drug use, opinions on the risks of drugs, and general demographic information. The raw information (which can be found HERE) has been converted into a usable dataset which can be found on Kaggle.
